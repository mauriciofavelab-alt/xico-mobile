# XICO Core

XICO is the current brand evolution of XICO → XICO.

It is a premium editorial product around Mexican culture in Spain and Europe.
It is not a generic content app, not a tourist guide, not an institutional portal, and not a superapp.

The product must preserve a productive tension:
- the earlier personalized editorial app logic
- the newer public-facing cultural publication logic

That tension is not a flaw. It is part of the product thesis.

## The real product question

The central product question is not:
"Is the interface beautiful?"

It is:
"Why would someone open XICO tomorrow?"

Long-form content alone is not enough.
A beautiful archive alone is not enough.
Good taste alone is not enough.

XICO needs ritual, recurrence, memory, and identity.

## What makes XICO defensible

XICO can become defensible if it combines:
- strong editorial voice
- culturally specific curation
- archive value
- identity mechanics that feel earned
- habit loops that do not feel childish
- access to stories, people, and scenes that are not replaceable by generic media

## The emotional center

Archivo is not a settings page.
Archivo is the emotional center of the product.

It should feel like:
- memory
- identity
- cultural self-definition
- belonging
- personal editorial history

## The strategic danger

The biggest danger is building something beautiful that people admire once and do not reopen.

XICO succeeds only if beauty, editorial intelligence, and repeat-opening mechanics reinforce each other.
